Salsa Chicken

Simple, Quick Slow Cooker Crockpot Chicken

This recipe is as easy as it gets! With just a few simple ingredients and a little bit of foresight, you can have a delicious dinner that the whole family will love. Your kids will rave about the flavor, you will rave about how "set-it-and-forget-it" easy this recipe is!

All you need to do is line your slow cooker with boneless, skinless chicken breasts. Then, simply sprinkle on a packet of your favorite taco seasoning, dump in a jar of your salsa of choice, squeeze in a little lime juice, and set your slow cooker to 4 hours on high (or 8 hours on low!). Dinner for the whole family in a few short steps!

When your slow cooker is finished, grab two forks and shred that juicy, delicious chicken. Mix in some shredded cheese to your heart's content, finish with some lime juice, and serve! This chicken goes great with tortillas, on chips for nachos, or even on it's own. Not to mention, the leftovers the next day reheat quite well!

Ingredients

  • Four boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • One packet of taco seasoning
  • One jar of salsa
  • Three cups of shredded Mexican cheese
  • One lime

Steps

  1. Line the slow cooker with your chicken breasts.
  2. Sprinkle the taco seasoning over the chicken.
  3. Dump the entire jar of salsa on top of the chicken.
  4. Squeeze juice of one half of a lime into the slow cooker.
  5. Cook four hours on high, or eight hours on low
  6. When the slow cooker is done cooking, take two forks and shred the chicken.
  7. Mix in some shredded Mexican cheese and the juice of the unused half of the lime.
  8. Serve on tortillas, over chips, or in your favorite dish!
